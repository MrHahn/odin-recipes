PB & J Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic and simple combination. It features peanut butter and jelly spread on slices of bread, offering a sweet and salty flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Bread (white, wheat, or your favorite)
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly (grape, strawberry, or your favorite)
  • Butter knife (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients: Make sure you have everything you need within reach.
  2. Take two slices of bread: Lay them flat on a plate or cutting board.
  3. Spread the peanut butter: Use a butter knife or the peanut butter jar to spread a layer of peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  4. Spread the jelly: On the other slice of bread, spread a layer of jelly.
  5. Combine the slices: Put the two slices of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly facing each other.
  6. Cut and enjoy: You can cut the sandwich in half diagonally for a classic look.
